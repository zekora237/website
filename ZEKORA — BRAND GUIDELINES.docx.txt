ZEKORA — BRAND GUIDELINES
(Internal Document — Version 1.0)
________________


1️. Purpose of This Document
This document defines how Zekora looks, sounds, and feels everywhere.
Goals:
* Ensure consistency across all platforms
* Make Zekora instantly recognizable
* Guide collaborators, designers, and developers
* Prevent random or emotional branding decisions
📌 This document evolves, but changes must be intentional.
________________


2️. Brand Essence
Brand Name
ZEKORA
Brand Meaning
A modern, abstract name representing:
* Precision
* Structure
* Technology
* African-rooted but globally scalable innovation
________________


Brand Positioning
“Zekora builds digital solutions that structure, modernize, and grow businesses — while also creating scalable tech products.”
________________


Brand Personality
Zekora is:
* Professional, not corporate
* Clear, not complicated
* Calm, not loud
* Confident, not arrogant
* Modern, not trendy
📌 If Zekora were a person:
A calm, intelligent engineer who explains complex things simply.
________________


3️. Target Audience (Brand Perspective)
Zekora speaks to:
* Small & medium businesses
* Hotels, motels, local enterprises
* Entrepreneurs & startups
* Organizations seeking digitalisation
* Future SaaS users
📌 Brand language must be understandable by non-technical people.
________________


4️. Brand Voice & Tone
Voice (Never changes)
* Clear
* Respectful
* Structured
* Solution-oriented
Tone (Adapts slightly)
Context
	Tone
	Client discussion
	Calm & reassuring
	Proposal / document
	Professional & structured
	Marketing
	Clear & confident
	Internal
	Direct & precise
	Words Zekora Likes
* Solution
* Structure
* Clarity
* Reliable
* Scalable
* Simple
Words Zekora Avoids
* Cheap
* Quick money
* Magic
* Guaranteed success
* Hype language
________________


5️. Visual Identity
🎨 Color Palette (Initial)
Primary Color
* Deep Tech Blue / Indigo
→ Trust, technology, stability
Secondary Colors
   * Soft Gray
→ Neutral, professional
   * White
→ Clarity, simplicity
Accent Color (Optional)
      * Muted Green or Teal
→ Growth, progress
📌 Avoid flashy neon colors.
________________


🅰 Typography
Primary Font (Suggested)
         * Inter
         * Poppins
         * Roboto
Rules
         * Sans-serif only
         * Clean and readable
         * No decorative fonts
________________


🧱 Layout Principles
         * White space is intentional
         * Content > decoration
         * Clear sections
         * Simple alignment
📌 If it looks “too simple”, it’s probably right.
________________


6️. Logo Guidelines (Conceptual for now)
Until a final logo is designed:
         * Use ZEKORA in uppercase
         * Sans-serif font
         * Neutral or dark color
         * No shadows, no gradients
📌 Logo must:
         * Work in black & white
         * Look professional even without color
         * Scale well (favicon → banner)
________________


7️. Brand Signature / Attribution
For client projects:
“Built by Zekora”
or
“Powered by Zekora”
📌 Subtle, professional, never intrusive.
________________


8️. Brand Behavior Rules
Zekora always:
✔ Explains before selling
✔ Sets clear expectations
✔ Documents decisions
✔ Delivers what is promised
Zekora never:
✖ Overpromises
✖ Copies competitors blindly
✖ Talks badly about other businesses
✖ Sacrifices quality for speed
________________


9️. Cultural & Ethical Foundations
         * Respect for people and businesses
         * Transparency in pricing and delivery
         * Long-term thinking
         * Ethical tech usage
📌 Zekora builds trust before revenue.
________________
